Windy River
This is called the Windy River here in Colorado! It is very fun and relaxing! It's great to take children on if you have any. The river runs through a couple of popular towns as well as Denver! Come join us for the time of your life on this lovely mediocre river!
FAQ:
Can this river tolerate my 5 year old?
Yes! This river is good for children of most ages, starting at 3 years old.

How long does it take to raft the Windy River?
Good question! It takes roughly 3 and a half hours, depending on how fast you paddle!

Has anyone ever died on this river?
No! Not yet anyway!

Is it actually Windy?
No
Milo's Waterway
We welcome you to Milo's exciting waterway! This river is not for the faint of heart! It is filled with rocks and boulders. It has twists and turns, and it even ends with a class 5 waterfall towards the end! If you are looking for a thrill, I invite you to join us this summer on this wonderous journey through, Milo's Waterway!
